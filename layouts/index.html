ALF Association


We are a registered non-profit and our goal is to support the Algorithms for Lattice Fermions (ALF) project.


We strive to obtain funding for the package's:
  • maintenance
  • improvement and
  • user base support and growth

The ALF package provides an open-source, high-performance, general code for auxiliary-field quantum Monte Carlo simulations.
