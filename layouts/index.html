ALF Association


We are a registered non-profit with the goal of promoting and further supporting the Algorithms for Lattice Fermions (ALF) project.

ALF is a state-of-the-art, high-performance quantum simulation package. You can read more about the open-source project here.

Contributions from members and donors ensure our continued support to ALF's:

  • Ease of use
  • User support
  • Community building
  • Continuous development
  • Education
See how we have been doing this here.
